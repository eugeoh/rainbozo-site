Rainbozo

Want the forecast without the clutter?

Rainbozo is a simple, distraction-free weather app, created out of frustration with overly complicated weather apps. We provide accurate and reliable forecasts, powered by trusted weather sources.

Key Features:

  • Clean, easy-to-read forecasts
  • Weekly weather forecast
  • 24-hour graph of rain weight (algorithm combining probability & rainfall in mm)
  • No ads, no clutter
  • Uses your current location, whether at home or work
  • Search and save up to three additional locations for faster forecasts
