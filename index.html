Rainbozo

Want the forecast without the clutter?

Rainbozo is a simple, distraction-free weather app, created out of frustration with overly complicated weather apps. We provide accurate and reliable forecasts, powered by trusted weather sources.

Key Features:

  • Clean, easy-to-read forecasts
  • Weekly weather forecast
  • 24-hour graph of rain weight (algorithm combining probability & rainfall in mm)
  • No ads, no clutter
  • Uses your current location, whether at home or work
  • Search and save up to three additional locations for faster forecasts
Privacy Policy

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Rainbozo collect my personal data?

No. Rainbozo does not collect or store any personal data. Your saved locations are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time.

Why does Rainbozo need my location?

We use your location to provide accurate, real-time weather forecasts. Your location is processed in real time and is never stored or shared.

Can I check the weather for multiple locations?

Yes, you can search and save up to three additional locations for quick access to their forecasts.
